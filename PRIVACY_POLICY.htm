Studiously Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026
Developer: Neil

1. Overview

Studiously respects your privacy. This app is designed to help users manage their exam revision timetables efficiently without collecting, storing, or transmitting any personal data.

2. Information Collection

Studiously does not collect, process, or share any personal information.

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are used.
  • No data is sent off your device to external servers.

3. Data Storage

All app data (such as your timetable entries or reminders) is stored locally on your device. It remains under your control and is deleted automatically if you uninstall the app.

4. Permissions

Studiously only requests permissions necessary for its core functionality (for example, notifications or local storage). These permissions are used solely on‑device and never for data collection.

5. Third‑Party Services

Studiously does not integrate with or rely on any third‑party services that access user data.

6. Children’s Privacy

Studiously is safe for all ages. It does not collect any information from children or adults.

7. Changes to This Policy

If future updates introduce new features that require data handling, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly. Users will be notified within the app before any changes take effect.
